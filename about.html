Holy Shepherd Educational Society (REGO), Bangalore

Founded in 1972 by Rev. S. Samuel, Holy Shepherd Educational Society has been a beacon of quality education, moral values, and discipline for over five decades. Our institution is committed to holistic learning, fostering intellectual, emotional, and physical development in every student. We believe in equal education for all, regardless of caste, creed, or social standing.

Our esteemed founder was a visionary and philanthropist, dedicated to accessible education. In his spirit, we continue to provide scholarships to deserving students, ensuring opportunities for academic and personal growth. At Holy Shepherd, we nurture a creative and inclusive environment, encouraging academic excellence, sports, and cultural activities to help students thrive.
