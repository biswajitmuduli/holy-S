Welcome to Holy Shepherd Educational Society (REGO), Bangalore

Founded in 1972 by Rev. S. Samuel, Holy Shepherd Educational Society has been a pioneer in quality education for over 53 years. As a prestigious Christian institution, we are dedicated to academic excellence, moral values, and discipline, ensuring education is accessible to all, regardless of caste, creed, or social standing.

Our mission is to nurture well-rounded individuals by fostering intellectual, emotional, and physical development. With a comprehensive curriculum, state-of-the-art facilities, and a commitment to holistic learning, we strive to empower students with knowledge, character, and leadership skills for a successful future.
